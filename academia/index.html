Research

Studying what light can reveal about the eye.

I’m entering my third year as an Optical Sciences PhD student in the University of Arizona’s Polarization Lab, advised by Dr. Meredith Kupinski and co-advised by Dr. Matthew Kupinski.

Current work

I develop a polarimetric model of the eye for studying diseases including keratoconus, cataracts, and retinal degeneration—work connecting optical engineering, ophthalmology, and computer graphics.
