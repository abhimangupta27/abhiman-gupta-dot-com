02 · Research

Using light to understand the living eye.

Right now, light is teaching me how to see the living eye differently.

One current thread sits at the intersection of optical engineering, ophthalmology, and computer graphics. I develop polarimetric eye models to study how tissue changes in conditions including keratoconus, cataracts, and retinal degeneration.
